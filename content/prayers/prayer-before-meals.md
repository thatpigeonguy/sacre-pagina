title: Prayer Before Meals
---

### English
Bless us, O Lord,  
and these, thy gifts,  
which we are about to receive  
from thy bounty.  
Through Christ our Lord.  

Amen.

### Latin

Benedic, Domine, nos
et haec tua dona
quae de tua benedictione sumimus.
Per Christum Dominum nostrum.
Amen.
