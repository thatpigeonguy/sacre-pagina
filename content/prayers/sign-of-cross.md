> **How to Pray:**
> Touch your forehead with your right hand, say "In the name of the Father." Touch your chest, say "and of the Son." Touch your left shoulder, then your right shoulder, say "and of the Holy Spirit." Clasp hands together, say "Amen."

### English
In the name of the Father,  
and of the Son,  
and of the Holy Spirit.  

Amen.

### Latin
In nomine Patris,  
et Filii,  
et Spiritus Sancti.  

Amen.
